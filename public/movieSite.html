Bretts Top 3

Movies That Stick In Mind

Evil Dead

the perfect mix of horror campiness, gore and spookiness

The Boy and The Beast

A beautifully animated movie about an orphan who studies under an animal like matial arts master. A coming of age story that hits different as a father

Twin Peaks: Fire Walk With Me

The movie that follows the original series of Twin Peaks takes a close look into Laura Palmers life before her death. Haunting and a must see for TP fans.
